IBM 2020

FIELD.io
Date : 2019 / 2020
A series of audio reactive animations for the digital IBM Think 2020 conference.

I worked as a Houdini artist for the duration of the project across the whole pipeline, starting with R&D for audio driven geometry creation to developing design iterations and eventually creating animations with the final system. I was also tasked with the majority of the rendering and post for the final stills and animations, using Photoshop and After Effects for the latter.
