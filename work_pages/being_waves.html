BEING WAVES

Studio ANRK
Date : 2022
A trailer for an upcoming music driven VR experience, funded by Arts Council England and the National Lottery.

Building a custom particle system I guided particles along an evolving network of splines, pulsing and fluctuating to the music. I was the sole 3D artist; responsible for RnD, animation, lighting, shading and rendering.

The resulting 30 second trailer and stills are used as the visual identity and to promote the project.

Still being used at a public testing event and video being used on the project website.

Work In Progress stills for exploring shading.

Project page: beingwaves.com
