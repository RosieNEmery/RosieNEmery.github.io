REGENERATIVE BY DESIGN

FIELD.io
Date : 2019
An internal project looking at future materials and their uses in a series of short films. The films I worked on looked at using honeycomb as a building material and a kinetic material as a microplastic filter in the sea.

I mainly worked as a Houdini technical artist, doing R&D for both films, creating the initial honeycomb structure and procedural bee system and the sea filter's simulation/animation. I also helped to translate simulation attributes into shader attributes for the sea filter film.

An early animation of the honeycomb building and the underlying bee system in the final animation which grows the honeycomb through an infections style spreading.

Full documentation at field.blue/explorations/regenerative-by-design
