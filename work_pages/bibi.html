WEAR THE WEEKEND

The Mill
Date : 2021
AR fashion filters for Samsung and musician Bibi(비비), in collaboration with 6 digital fashion designers. The filters were built in Snapchat's Lens Studio.

I worked on the shading and texturing for 4 of the designs, creating assets in Houdini, TouchDesigner and Photoshop. I also did clean-up, uv mapping and retopo of models when needed.

Designers Ines Alpha and Maison Taskin modelling filters based on their design. Most of the shading for the filters was done procedurally using GLSL based shading techniques.

Designs by Ines Alpha, Gwen Kim/Jak 3D, Linxi Zhu, Maison Taskin, Adiella Bachshe/SGI and Aliina Kauranne. I worked on Ines', Gwen's, Maison's and Adiella's designs.

Videos sourced from Instagram from designers above and @nakedbibi

Official Samsung launch: www.samsung.com/uk/explore/brand/try-on-digital-fashion-with-ready-to-wear-ar-filters
