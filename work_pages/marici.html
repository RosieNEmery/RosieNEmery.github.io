MARICI

Personal Project
Date : 2023
A project started as an exercise in live action integration.

I was responsible for all CGI elements, comp, cut and sound. The project was completed in Blender, Houdini, Nuke and Resolve with rendering done in Redshift.

Render of the final design; the design is inspired by south asian bridal headpieces and the S/S21 Iris Van Herpen collection.

(Above) 3D scans taken of the environment and head model to help with intergration. (Bottom Right) Vellum simulation of the chains, using packed spheres for collisions. (Bottom Left) Animation of a single armature which was then instanced and offset for the final design.

(1)Raw footage (2)Tracking in Blender (3)Tracked head animation and headpiece (4)Shadow pass comp (5)Final headpiece render (6) Final comp + colour in Nuke
