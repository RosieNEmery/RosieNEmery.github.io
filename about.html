Rosie Emery is a digital & 3D artist based in London.

Since completing her studies in Computer Animation and Visualisation in 2017, she has gone on to work as a technical artist/3D artist; developing her skills in Houdini and learning webgl and TouchDesigner along the way.

Rosie is now currently working as a freelance Houdini technical artist/3D Artist.
