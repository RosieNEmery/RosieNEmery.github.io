Rosie Emery is a digital 3D artist based in London.

Since completing her studies in Computer Animation and Visualisation in 2017, she has gone on to work as a 3D artist & technical artist; developing her skills in Houdini and learning webgl and AR design along the way.

Rosie is now currently working as a freelance 3D artist/technical artist.
